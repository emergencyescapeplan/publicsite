About

Emergency Escape Plan is an instrumental and electronic music formation, created by French composer and bass player, Jordi Authier, in Toulouse 2023, after meeting independent producer and ‘Ableton wizard’ of Jamiroquai, Howard Whiddett (AKA Sandancer). From this meeting, two worlds collided to create one, mixing Whiddett's subtle and powerful electronic production with Jordi’s highly arranged compositions, incorporating influences from a lot of genres, culminating in poetic, intense, eclectic and organic electronic music.

Emergency Escape Plan also involves collaboration from many talented artists, working with a new generation of French talents, amongst them the jazz sax player Charlie Burg, and pop/soul singer Octave Lissner.

Fans of Bonobo, Cinematic Orchestra, Thom Yorke, and Air will enjoy the combination of the infectious rippling rhythms and delicate melodies with the darker grooves and the harsher electronic sounds.
